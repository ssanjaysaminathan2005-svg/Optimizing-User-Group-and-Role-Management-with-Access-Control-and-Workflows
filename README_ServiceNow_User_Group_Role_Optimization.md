## üìò Project Overview
This project focuses on **optimizing user, group, and role management** within the **ServiceNow platform** by designing and implementing a robust **access control framework** and **automated workflows**.  
The initiative aims to improve **security**, **compliance**, and **operational efficiency** through **role-based access control (RBAC)**, **workflow automation**, and **integration with identity management systems**.

---

## üöÄ Key Objectives
- Streamline **user and group management** within ServiceNow.  
- Implement **role-based access control (RBAC)** aligned with organizational policies.  
- Automate **onboarding, offboarding, and access request** workflows.  
- Ensure **security, compliance, and audit readiness**.  
- Enhance **system performance and scalability**.  
- Integrate with **external systems** such as HR and identity management platforms (e.g., Azure AD, Okta).

---

## üß± Project Phases

### 1. **Induction Phase**
Introduces the project scope, stakeholders, and objectives.  
Focuses on familiarizing the team with existing ServiceNow configurations and preparing the foundation for execution.


---

### 2. **Requirement Analysis**
Captures business, functional, and non-functional requirements for optimizing user, group, and role management.

---

### 3. **Design Phase**
Defines the architecture, data flow, and workflow automation logic within ServiceNow.  
Covers RBAC design, ACL rules, and integration blueprints.


---

### 4. **Implementation Phase**
Configuration and development within ServiceNow using **Flow Designer**, **ACLs**, and **Business Rules**.  
Includes role restructuring, workflow creation, and security settings configuration.


---

### 5. **Performance and Testing Phase**
Validates system reliability, scalability, and compliance through structured testing methods such as **unit**, **integration**, **UAT**, and **performance testing**.

---

### 6. **Deployment and Continuous Improvement**
The final solution is deployed to production with structured change management, user training, and monitoring mechanisms.
